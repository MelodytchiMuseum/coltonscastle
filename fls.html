Frontline Strike is a unique strategy game in which you build and train an army before taking down your enemies! Featured is a single-player campaign where you can choose to ally yourself with one of three armies (or be independent) and free battles supporting up to 4 players. The game features both computer-controlled players and online multiplayer, as well as split-screen co-op. There are a variety of modes to play aside from the Standard mode, such as Onslaught and King of the Hill! You can even create your own maps to play on, and share them with friends. Frontline Strike also has a tutorial to help new players learn how to play.
